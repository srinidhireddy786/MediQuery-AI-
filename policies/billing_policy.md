# Hospital Billing and Financial Policy

**Document ID:** HP-BIL-2026  
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Approved By:** Finance and Billing Department  

## 1. Billing Statement Overview
Billing statements are generated and sent to patients within 14 business days of discharge. A final consolidated statement includes:
*   Room charges (calculated per night).
*   Physician and professional service fees.
*   Laboratory, imaging, and diagnostic test charges.
*   Medication and pharmacy charges administered during the stay.
*   Medical supplies and equipment fees.

## 2. Payment Terms and Co-pays
*   **Co-payments:** Co-payments and deductibles required by the patient’s insurance provider must be paid at the time of check-in for elective procedures. For emergency admissions, co-pays will be processed prior to discharge.
*   **Payment Due Date:** Full payment or an approved payment plan setup is required within 30 calendar days from the statement date.
*   **Payment Methods:** We accept major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), bank transfers, personal checks, and approved health savings accounts (HSA/FSA).

## 3. Financial Assistance and Discounts
*   **Prompt Payment Discount:** A 10% discount is applied to the self-pay portion of the bill if paid in full within 7 business days of receiving the statement.
*   **Financial Assistance Program (Charity Care):** Patients with household incomes below 200% of the Federal Poverty Level (FPL) are eligible for a 100% write-off of their outstanding balance. Patients between 201% and 400% of the FPL may qualify for a sliding scale discount ranging from 50% to 80%.
*   **Payment Plans:** Interest-free payment plans are available for balances exceeding $500, with terms extending up to 24 months depending on the total amount.
